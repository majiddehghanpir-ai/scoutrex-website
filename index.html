Under the Hood

How the AI engine works

Four layers of intelligence that turn raw data into decisions you can trust.

01

Deep Profile Parsing

We extract structured signals from resumes, work history, and stated preferences, building a multi-dimensional profile that goes far beyond keywords.

02

Semantic Role Mapping

Job descriptions are parsed with the same depth, understanding what a role actually requires, the team culture, and growth potential.

03

Multi-Signal Matching

Skills, culture, salary band, location, trajectory, and growth stage are all weighted and scored together, producing a single match confidence score.

04

Continuous Learning

Every interaction, applications, hires, rejections, and feedback, feeds back into the model, making every match sharper over time.
